C. Geographic Distribution
Gaza: High concentration of technical roles (PalTel, Massar, Nafith).
Ramallah: Hub for FinTech (Bank of Palestine, Palestine Stock Exchange) and High-tech (ASAL, Exalt).
Hebron: Dominated by Palestine Polytechnic University and Nafith Logistics.
Jenin: Exclusively Arab American University internships.
Remote Work: Approximately 46% of the internships offer a remote work option.
D. Skills Demand (Top 5 Required)
Python: The most dominant language, crossing Data Science, Web, and Automation.
SQL: Essential for almost all Data, Business Intelligence, and Backend roles.
Java: Heavy usage in Banking (FinTech) and Enterprise software.
React: The go-to library for Frontend and Mobile (React Native).
Docker/Kubernetes: High demand due to the prevalence of Cloud/DevOps roles.
E. Field Trends
Dominant Fields: Software Engineering, Data Science, and FinTech.
Niche Fields: "Artificial Intelligence" and "Cybersecurity" are emerging strongly, particularly with companies like ASAL and Exalt.

Detailed Breakdown
Who is hiring?
PalTel Group and Bank of Palestine are the volume leaders.
ASAL Technologies and Exalt Technologies focus heavily on specialized engineering (AI/ML and Cyber).
Universities (AAU, PPU) act as major employers in their respective cities (Jenin, Hebron), likely focusing on research and academic-adjacent tech roles.
Where is the money?
Exalt Technologies and ASAL Technologies appear to have competitive stipends, likely due to the specialized nature of the work (Cybersecurity/AI).
FinTech roles (Bank of Palestine, Palestine Stock Exchange) offer stable, mid-to-high range stipends.
Note: The stipend data shows high variance even within the same company, suggesting that pay is heavily dependent on the specific role (e.g., a "Digital Forensics" intern is paid more than a general "Web Support" intern).
Skill Clusters
Data Science Cluster: Python, SQL, Pandas, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow.
Web/Mobile Cluster: React, Node.js, JavaScript, Flutter, Swift, MongoDB.
Enterprise/Backend Cluster: Java, Spring Boot, Docker, AWS, SQL.
Embedded/Hardware Cluster: C/C++, Raspberry Pi, Arduino, Embedded Linux (Specific to PPU).
Recommendation for Applicants
Gaza Applicants: Focus on Cloud Networking, Python, and Java. PalTel and Massar are the key targets here.
Ramallah Applicants: Focus on FinTech (Blockchain, Financial Modeling) and CyberSecurity.
Hebron/Jenin Applicants: Academic research skills (Matlab, Latex) and Embedded Systems are highly valued in these regions due to the universities' influence.
Skill Up: Learning Docker and AWS significantly increases eligibility across multiple high-paying companies.
